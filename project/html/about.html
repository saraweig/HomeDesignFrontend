ABOUT US

Stunning artwork, eye-catching ornaments and handy storage options; welcome to our wonderland. Feast your eyes on our latest collection of home accessories, with beautiful pieces designed for every room of the house. Whatever your style, color palette and budget, find the perfect addition for living rooms, bedrooms and more; fill empty sideboards and fireplaces with clusters of colorful vases, candles and photo frames, add a sparkle to feature walls with Deco-inspired mirrors and modern canvases,and bring the beauty of nature into your space with artificial flowers in every color of the rainbow.

HOW IT ALL STARTED . . .

Our company was born somewhere in 2003 out of a genuine desire to make the unique ornamental products that we design and import accessible to the public. In recent years we have specialized in the import and marketing of ornamental products wholesale to the business community nationwide such as nurseries, shops, galleries and more. And so after many years of work we realized that there are those who connect with us, our heart, our taste.

WHAT IS HAPPENING TODAY . . .

Today, our customers have at their disposal a large selection of imported products in a variety of designs, styles and materials, suitable for a wide range of needs starting from upgrading and renewing the appearance of the home and garden, creating a magical atmosphere in private. and business events, to personal gifts for holidays and special events. At the beginning of 2019, we embarked on a process aimed at allowing the private audience to also enjoy beautiful and unique products that we design, at particularly attractive prices. We have created an online store for online orders that are delivered to your home with full security. We certainly understand the difficulty and concerns in choosing furniture online, that's why we always try to provide the most accurate product details combined with photos that clearly reflect reality. In addition, we give a full guarantee to those who chose us!
